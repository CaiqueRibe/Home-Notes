## [VSC Shorcuts for Windows](https://code.visualstudio.com/shortcuts/keyboard-shortcuts-windows.pdf)

Ctrl+K Ctrl+O --> Open Folder   
Ctrl+R --> Open Recent  
Ctrl+` --> Toggle Terminal
